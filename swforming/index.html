Electric & Energy

Racking & Shelving​

Racking & shelving system includes upright racking, box & step beam, racking beam and racking shelf panel. Sunway Machinery can offer a full upright system.

READ MORE

Door & Window​​

Sunway Machinery is committed to the door and window roll forming machines, including rolling shutter slat for door& window,octagonal pipe, guide rail, door & window frame roll forming machines.

READ MORE

Steel Structure

Steel structures are metal structures that are interconnected by structural steel members to carry loads and provide sufficient rigidity. It includes the drywall, purlin, deck floor, scaffolding plank and the container beam roll forming machines.

READ MORE

Metal Wall & Roofing

It includes roof panel, glazed tile panel, metal deck panel, standing seam panel, gutter and ridge capping. Its products are widely used in roof and wall system in workshop construction and housing construction.
